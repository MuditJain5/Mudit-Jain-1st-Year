Why learn Front End Web Development ?

The front-end developer creates interaction and user experience with scripts embedded in a site’s HTML. Everything a visitor to a website sees, clicks, or uses to input or retrieve information is the work of the front-end developer who creates client-side software that brings the site’s design to life.Everything on the front end is built with a mix of HTML, CSS, and client-side scripts like JavaScript—the core elements of front-end development. The front-end developer bridges the world of design and technology, bringing design to life and packaging up the utility of the back end in a modern, inviting way for users to interact with.
